What Drives Me

The core principles behind every line of code and design decision

User-First Mindset

Every feature starts with a simple question: "How can this make someone's work easier?" I believe technology should adapt to people, not the other way around.

Never Stop Learning

Being a student means embracing curiosity. I'm constantly exploring new technologies, design patterns, and user feedback to make Product Compass better.

Quality Over Quantity

I'd rather build one feature that works beautifully than ten that are mediocre. Clean code, thoughtful design, and reliable performance are non-negotiable.
