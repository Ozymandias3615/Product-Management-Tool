Features

Explore the powerful capabilities of Product Compass in greater detail.

Drag-and-Drop Interface

Our drag-and-drop interface empowers you to sculpt your roadmap effortlessly, allowing you to select, group, and reorder features in bulk with ease. Migrate items across statuses or releases with a single gesture, see your changes reflected instantly in all views, and maintain team alignment through real-time synchronization.

Perfect for product managers who need to quickly reorganize sprint priorities during planning meetings.

Milestone Tracking

Milestone tracking goes beyond static dates by allowing you to attach multiple milestones to feature groups, set dependencies to visualize critical paths, and enable automatic notifications. Monitor progress with percentage bars and alerts for looming deadlines to ensure stakeholder alignment and on-time delivery.

Ideal for project managers coordinating multiple teams across different time zones and release cycles.

Roadmap Views

Choose from dynamic timeline, quarterly summary, or fully customizable Gantt charts to visualize project plans. Zoom in on sprint cycles or zoom out for a comprehensive portfolio view, apply filters to focus on specific releases, highlight key milestones for stakeholder clarity, and export high-quality snapshots for presentations or reporting.

Used by executives to present quarterly plans to the board and by teams to track sprint progress.

Agile Board & Tasks

Transform your roadmap into an Agile powerhouse with Kanban boards that let you define custom columns, enforce WIP limits, assign tasks with deadlines and checklists, and leverage sprint planning modes to track velocity and throughput. Visualize progress in real-time and identify bottlenecks quickly to maintain smooth workflows.

Essential for scrum masters running daily standups and sprint retrospectives with distributed teams.

Collaboration & Feedback

Foster seamless collaboration with direct comments on roadmap items, tagging teammates for feedback and updates. Vote on feature importance to align priorities democratically, integrate user feedback portals to capture suggestions and bugs, and link feedback to roadmap entries for end-to-end traceability.

Used by agile teams to vote on roadmap changes in real-time and by support teams to track feature requests.

Customer Insights

Craft comprehensive customer personas using demographic, behavioral, and usage data. Visualize user journeys to uncover touchpoints and pain points, attach qualitative research notes and feedback for depth, rank personas by impact scores to guide prioritization, and ensure your roadmap aligns with real-world user needs.

Valuable for UX researchers documenting user interviews and product teams validating feature ideas.

Shareable & Exportable

Share your roadmap effortlessly with secure, branded public links or embed live views on external websites for real-time updates. Download customizable PDF, PNG, and CSV exports with your branding, adjust role-based access controls for stakeholder-specific views, and track link usage and feedback with built-in analytics.

Perfect for sharing roadmap updates with clients and generating reports for quarterly reviews.
